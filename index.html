00 — Philosophy

Seven layers, one feeling

This vibe isn't a single trick. It's the disciplined stacking of seven layers. Miss one and it collapses into "generic". Stack all seven and it reads premium, every time.

01

Black is the canvas

Backgrounds are near-pure black (#050509). Never a purple fill. Darkness is what makes the light glow.

02

Violet is light, not paint

Purple appears as neon rim-light, edge glow and atmosphere — emitted, never coated.

03

Type towers & layers

One giant display word fills 60–70% of the frame, layered front & behind the subject.

04

3D is conceptual

A single hyperreal object that means something — a maze, a staircase, a statue.

05

Glass is the surface

UI lives on frosted translucent panels: blur, thin bright border, soft inner glow.

06

Texture adds depth

Perspective grids, grain, starfields, blurred mesh-orbs. Never a flat empty black.

07

Motion breathes

Float, glow-pulse, hover-lift, light-sweep. Subtle, slow, alive.

✕ OLD / GENERIC

Flat purple boxes. Centered text. Stock icons. Even grid of cards. Feels like a template.

✓ NEW / CINEMATIC

Black depth. Glowing concept 3D. Towering type. Glass panels. Texture & light. Feels like a film poster.

Don't

06 — 3D Imagery

The cinematic hero object

Each visual centers on one hyperreal 3D object that means something. This is the direction sheet — subjects, lighting, materials, framing. (When you need an actual generation prompt, I'll write it from these rules.)

SUBJECT LIBRARY
  • Marble / chrome statue (wisdom, authority)
  • Astronaut (exploration, frontier)
  • Glowing staircase (growth, scale)
  • Maze / labyrinth (the problem)
  • Glass crown · diamond (premium, value)
  • Brain · circuit (intelligence, AI)
  • Chess piece · hourglass (strategy, urgency)
LIGHTING
  • Single violet key-light, hard shadow
  • Neon rim-light tracing the silhouette
  • Reflective wet floor catching the glow
  • Volumetric haze / god-rays
  • Background falls to pure black
MATERIAL & MOOD
  • Frosted glass, chrome, polished marble
  • Neon acrylic, brushed metal
  • Cinematic depth-of-field, shallow focus
  • Hyperreal render, 8k, octane feel
  • Dramatic, premium, quiet confidence
FRAMING

Subject centered or 1/3. Giant type behind the subject's top, smaller type in front of its base (occlusion = depth). Negative space breathes. Logo top-corner. Meta-label + page number as chrome.

Concept > decoration: pick the object for its meaning first. "Checkout is a maze", "growth is a staircase". The metaphor is what makes it stop the scroll.
